A universe made of love

If I say I love your eyes,
then what about your smile?

If I say I love your smile,
then what about your laughter?

If I say I love your laughter,
then what about your heart?

If I say I love your heart,
then what about your soul?

So no...

I can never have a favorite thing about you.

Because every part of you is my favorite—
today, tomorrow, and after 10,000 years.

And if I had the choice to choose you,
I would choose you every time.
